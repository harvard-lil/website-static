Collaborate

You're invited

LIL is a hallway connecting three rooms of party guests - law folks, tech folks, and library folks. It's a fantastic group of people and we want you to join us!

We invite you to collaborate with us anytime by dropping us an email or by sharing some code. We also hope you'll consider joining us in one of the roles below.

Caselaw Access Project Fellow

Harvard Law School invites applications to serve as the Caselaw Access Project Fellow during the 2017-18 year. Drawing on and contributing to the Law School's Caselaw Access Project, the Fellow will explore technology that enhances access to and understanding of the law and improves access to justice.
